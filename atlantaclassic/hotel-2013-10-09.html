 All reservations must be received on or before May 15, 2009 to receive the Atlanta Classic Group Rate of $79.00 plus tax. Reservation requests received after the cut-off date will be accepted on a space and rate availability basis up to the group room block. If the group rate is not available after the cut-off date, the prevailing rack rates will apply for any reservation confirmed.

 Please contact Susanne Farrington (after May 15) at 678-686-0416, Ext. 599. You must call her directly to get the group rate. You will not be able to get the group rate through the 800 number or the Front Desk of the DoubleTree. Please note the Atlanta Classic Meet Rate will not be guaranteed after May 15th.

Distances fron the hotel to Georgia Tech is 4.5 miles.
