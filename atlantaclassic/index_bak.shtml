W E L C O M E
to the Second Annual DeKalb International Prep Classic.
The 2005 edition of the “DeKalb Classic” will again offer quality competition for both women and men high school athletes.
The 2004 meet at Georgia Tech produced national rankings in a number of events. The limited number of entries per event, few heats or trials, start late-finish early schedule, no entry fee, no team scoring all contribute in providing a “go for it” atmosphere of friendly competition for athletes,
coaches and fans.

States represented in 2004:
Alabama, Florida, Kentucky, Georgia, New York,
North Carolina, South Carolina.
International:
Egypt.


Meet sanctioned by USA Track & Field
&
Approved by the NFHS


Major changes for 2005 are:

New date is Saturday 28 May 2005

One mile run replaced with the 1500m

Women’s 300mH replaced with the 400mH
