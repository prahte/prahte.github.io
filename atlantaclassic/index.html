W E L C O M E

May 24, 2008

FIFTH Annual
Atlanta-DeKalb International Prep Classic

The 2007 Edition of the "DeKalb Classic" offered quality competition for both women and men high school athletes.

See our 2004 to 2007 "DeKalb Classic"

Women's & Men's Records
(74k Adobe Acrobat PDF)

The 2007 "DeKalb Classic" at Georgia Tech produced national rankings in a number of events. The limited number of entries per event, few heats or trials, start late-finish early schedule, no entry fee, no team scoring all contribute in providing
a "go for it" atmosphere of friendly competition
for athletes, coaches and fans.

U.S. States represented to date:
Alabama, Florida, Kentucky, Georgia, Maryland, Mississippi, New York, North Carolina, South Carolina, Tennessee, Texas

16 Countries represented to date:
Bahamas, Belgium, Brazil, British Virgin Islands,
Cayman Islands, Chile, Egypt, El Salvador, Guatemala, Guyana, Jamaica, Netherlands Antilles, Nigeria, Somalia, Sudan, United States

Meet sanctioned by USA Track & Field
&
Approved by the NFHS
